what we do

We build games that channel AAA polish into bite sized experiences meant to delight.

our story

It all started with a desire to build a new generation of games that embrace all the conflicts that engulf the industry and use those as an advantage rather than a trap. We embrace the tension between humanity and algorithm and between simplicity and high art and choose to find ways to fit a product inside these pillars.
