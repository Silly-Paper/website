Last Updated: June 23, 2025Your privacy is important to Silly Paper and we take our responsibility of caring for it seriously. This policy describes how Silly Paper Inc. ("Silly Paper") processes the personal information we collect about you when you use our products and services or when you attend live events hosted by or in connection with Silly Paper (collectively, "Services" or "Silly Paper Services"). In some cases, our subsidiaries may be considered data controllers, either together with Silly Paper or by themselves.
1. Information We Collect
The information we collect depends on what Services you use and how you use them.
A. Information You Give Us Directly
ⓘ We collect information that you give us directly, such as when you create an Silly Paper Account, set up security questions, make purchases, or contact Silly Paper Help.
We collect information that you provide us, such as:
• Your Silly Paper Account information, including your email address, username, real name, country, date of birth, telephone number and password;
• Security questions and answers;
• Information you provide in response to surveys about our Services;
• Information you provide when seeking help from us, such as your name, telephone number, and records (including call recordings) of the issues you experience; and
• Billing information, such as your name, payment card number, payment account details, and shipping address.
